Privacy Policy for VFIED

Last updated: September 29, 2025

1. Information We Collect

VFIED collects minimal information to provide food recommendations:

  • Location data (city/country only) for restaurant recommendations
  • Dietary preferences (stored locally on your device)
  • Food mood inputs (not stored on servers)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide personalized food recommendations
  • Suggest nearby restaurants and events
  • Remember your dietary preferences

3. Data Storage

All personal preferences are stored locally on your device using browser storage. We do not transmit personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

VFIED may use third-party APIs for restaurant data and location services. These services have their own privacy policies.

5. Your Rights

You can delete your local data at any time by clearing your browser storage or uninstalling the app.

6. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact: support@vfied.io
